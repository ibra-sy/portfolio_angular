How AI is Changing Modern Cyber Attacks

2026
by SYLLA SCHEICKNA IBRAHIM

Cyber attackers now use AI to scan the internet faster, generate phishing emails that look perfectly human and chain together tools that can exploit known vulnerabilities in minutes. As defenders, we must automate too: patch quickly, monitor logs with ML, and never rely only on passwords.

Securing AI Tools Before They Become a Backdoor

2026
by SYLLA SCHEICKNA IBRAHIM

Many teams connect ChatGPT-like tools directly to their internal systems without strong access control. If an attacker steals these AI credentials, they can read sensitive data, send malicious prompts or trigger dangerous actions. Principle of least privilege, auditing tokens and isolating AI agents are now basic security requirements.
